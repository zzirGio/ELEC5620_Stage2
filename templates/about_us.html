About us

Daintree

Daintree is a sentiment analysis platform which aim to contribute in the stock market and the investment process. It is a platform to connect Companies to the opinion of the customers, in order to improve their product and company performance. The platform also offer an accessible mean for the (potential) investors to get a better understanding and insight of the products and companies they invested in or wish to invest into.
